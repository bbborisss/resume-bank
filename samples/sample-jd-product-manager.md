**Northwind Labs — Senior Product Manager, Growth**
San Francisco, CA (hybrid) · Full-time

Northwind Labs builds collaboration software used by 40,000 teams. We're hiring a Senior PM to own our self-serve growth funnel — acquisition, activation, and expansion.

**What you'll do**
- Own the roadmap for signup, onboarding, and upgrade flows end to end
- Design and run A/B experiments across the funnel; build the experimentation cadence
- Partner with engineering, design, and data science to ship weekly
- Define and instrument funnel metrics; drive decisions from cohort and retention analysis
- Identify and build product-led growth loops (referrals, sharing, team invites)

**What we're looking for**
- 5+ years of product management experience, 2+ in growth or consumer/PLG products
- Strong analytical skills: SQL, cohort analysis, experiment design and statistics
- Track record of shipping experiments that moved activation or conversion metrics
- Excellent written communication and stakeholder management
- Nice to have: experience with pricing/packaging, lifecycle marketing (email/push), or international expansion
