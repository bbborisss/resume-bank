**Copperleaf Robotics — Marketing Manager, Demand Generation**
Austin, TX (on-site 3 days/week) · Full-time

**What you'll do**
- Own paid and organic acquisition: search, LinkedIn, industry publications, SEO
- Build and run the email nurture program in HubSpot; own lead scoring with sales
- Produce campaign content with our product marketer: landing pages, case studies, webinars
- Report on pipeline contribution, CAC, and channel ROI; reallocate budget monthly
- Run our presence at 4–6 industry trade shows per year

**What we're looking for**
- 4+ years in B2B demand generation or growth marketing
- Hands-on with a marketing automation platform (HubSpot, Marketo) and Google/LinkedIn Ads
- Comfortable owning a budget and defending channel decisions with data
- Strong copywriting; you can ship a landing page without a committee
- Nice to have: marketing to operations/logistics buyers, ABM experience, basic SQL
